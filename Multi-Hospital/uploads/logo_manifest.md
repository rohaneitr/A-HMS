### Image Asset Preparation Guidelines
1. **Aspect Ratio:** The layout expects a ratio of `4.97:1` (approx. `5:1`). Any significant deviation may cause compression or overflowing in the navigation panels.
2. **Transparency:** High-quality alpha-transparency (`PNG-24`) is required to avoid solid white background blocks when rendering on dark layout headers or sidebars.
3. **Optimized Scaling:** If the logo is larger, pre-scale it down to `826x166` exactly using bicubic filtering to avoid browser-rendered aliasing.
